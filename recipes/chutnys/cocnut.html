Telugu Ruchulu

తెలుగు రుచులు

CoconutChutny/

కొబ్బరిచట్నీ

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 2 tbsp roasted chana dal (Bengal gram)
  • 1 green chili (adjust as per taste)
  • ½ inch ginger
  • 2 tbsp curd (optional, for extra creaminess)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

    • For tempering (optional but enhances flavor)

  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • dried red chili
  • A few curry leaves
  • 1 tsp oil

Instructions:

  1. 1. Blend grated coconut, roasted chana dal, green chili, ginger, salt, and curd (if using) with some water into a smooth paste.
  2. Adjust consistency by adding more water if needed. Transfer to a bowl.
  3. Prepare tempering: Heat oil in a small pan, add mustard seeds, urad dal, dried red chili, and curry leaves. Let them splutter.
  4. Pour tempering over the chutney and mix well.
  5. Serve with idli, dosa, or vada. Enjoy!
